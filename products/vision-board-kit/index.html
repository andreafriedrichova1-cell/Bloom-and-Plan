✦ Welcome ✦
Hello, Dreamer

You are holding something powerful. This Vision Board Kit is your sacred space to dream bigger, set intentions, and call in the life you truly deserve. There are no rules here — only possibility.

Take your time with each page. Light a candle, make yourself a cup of tea, and give yourself the gift of dreaming freely.

How To Use This Kit
01
Print Your Pages
Print on white cardstock (160–200gsm recommended) for a premium feel. Use standard A4 or US Letter paper for everyday printing.
02
Create Your Vision Board
Gather magazines, printed photos, or print images from online. Cut and collage them into the photo grid. Add your word of the year.
03
Set Your Intentions
Work through the goal planner and journal prompts slowly. Write in pen, answer honestly, and dream without limits.
04
Cut Your Affirmation Cards
Cut along the card borders and place them on your desk, mirror, journal, or car. Read them daily. Let them sink in.
